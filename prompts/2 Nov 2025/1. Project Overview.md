\# Project Title: Maven Fuzzy Factory E-Commerce Analytics



\## Objective

Analyze and optimize the online retail performance of a teddy bear e-commerce store by leveraging detailed marketing, website session, order, and product data.



\## Key Features

\- Trend analysis of website sessions and order volumes.

\- Calculation and tracking of session-to-order conversion rates.

\- Evaluation of marketing channel effectiveness.

\- Revenue performance analysis per order and per session.

\- User behavior insights based on session and purchase data.

\- Product popularity and returns analysis.



\## Technology Stack

\- Microsoft Fabric: Lakehouse for data storage, Data Pipeline for ETL workflows.

\- Power BI: Interactive dashboards and reports for business insights.

\- GitHub: Version control and collaboration.

\- Automation tools: To schedule refresh and deployment of data pipelines and reports.



\## Status

\- Current phase: Data exploration and pipeline design.

\- Upcoming milestones: Data ingestion setup, ETL pipeline creation, dashboard development, and automation deployment.
